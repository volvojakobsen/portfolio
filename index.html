Auction-Bay

Description

this is a project made at the end of my 3rd semester. It is a auction site where the users can register an account and get 1000 credits when registered. They can then use the credits to place a bid on items and they can also put an item of their own up for auction. The items they put up for auction can also be updated or deleted.

Holidaze

Description

This is a bnb-ish site where the users can browse venues for renting and staying at. On this site the users can register as either a regular customer or a venue manager. Regular customers can select available dates and book a stay at a venue. Venue managers can rent out their venues at this site, they can also update and delete their venues.
